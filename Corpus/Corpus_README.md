The Sister Book (E) from the St. Agnes Convent in Emmerich am Rhein is available in the critical edition by Anne Bollmann and Nikolaus Staubach: [Schwesternbuch und Statuten des St. Agnes-Konvents in Emmerich](https://search.worldcat.org/title/43221567). The digitized copy of the 20th-century transcription of the now-lost manuscript is available through the [Emmerich City Archives](https://www.emmerich.de/stadtarchiv#).

Plain Text files are available here for the _viten_ from the Master Geert's House Sister Book (G) and the Diepenveen Sister Book (D), as well as one _vite_ from the Lamme van Dies House Sister Book (the _vite_ of Andries Yserens). All of these texts are also freely available for download in a variety of formats (including XML) at the DBNL (De Digitale Bibliotheek voor de Nederlandse Letteren):
[Master Geert's House Sister Book (G)](https://www.dbnl.org/tekst/_hie005hier02_01/)
[Diepenveen Sister Book (D)](https://www.dbnl.org/tekst/_van005vand02_01/)
[Lamme van Dies House _vite_ of Andries Yserens](https://www.dbnl.org/tekst/_lev016leve01_01/)
